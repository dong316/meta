Alpha diversity analysis over time points

GLM model & LMM model

We use a General Linear Model for our non-normally distributed metrics. We change the distribution from Gaussian (normal) distribution to Quasipoisson (log) distribution that fits the data better. We also use Linear Mixed-effect model (LMM) to fit our data.

Faith

# Gaussian (normal) distribution
gaussian.faith.time = glm(faith_pd ~ age_day + paper, data=meta, family="gaussian")
plot(gaussian.faith.time, which=c(1,2))
# Quasipoisson (log) distribution
qp.faith.time = glm(faith_pd ~ age_day + study, data=meta, family="quasipoisson")
plot(qp.faith.time, which=c(1,2))
# linear mixed model
faith.mixed <- lmer(faith_pd ~ age_day + (1|paper), data = meta)
plot(faith.mixed)
summary(faith.mixed)
